Web Page Canvas v1.0.4

This is a free-to-use extension that I started as a hobby. It requires less than 1MB of storage and allows you to draw lines on your current web page.

I am a Computer Science student at City College, International Faculty of the University of Sheffield. I enjoy developing websites and plugins in my free time. If you have found any problems or have any ideas about the extension you can email me at damgiankakis@gmail.com.

The extension does not work on pdf files and on chrome's default pages.
